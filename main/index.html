Artist

Weeknd

The Weeknd, born Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He first gained recognition with his mixtapes in 2011 and has since released several critically acclaimed albums, including "Beauty Behind the Madness" in 2015 and "After Hours" in 2020.

Post Malone

Post Malone is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter known for his unique style of blending different genres such as hip hop, rock, and country. He gained recognition with his debut single "White Iverson" in 2015 and has since released three studio albums, with the most recent being "Hollywood's Bleeding" in 2019.

Twenty One Pilots

Twenty One Pilots is an American musical duo formed in 2009, consisting of Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun. The duo is known for their eclectic mix of alternative rock, hip-hop electronic music, as well as their energetic live performances. They gained mainstream recognition with their fourth studio album "Blurryface" in 2015.
